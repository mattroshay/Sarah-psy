## What burnout actually is

[DRAFT — Sarah to write] Burnout is a clinical state of chronic workplace stress that has not been successfully managed. It is not laziness, weakness, or just needing a holiday.

## Five signs you are burning out, not just tired

[DRAFT — Sarah to write] Emotional exhaustion, cynicism, reduced efficacy, physical symptoms, and the inability to switch off — these five signs together point toward burnout rather than ordinary fatigue.

## Why high performers are most at risk

[DRAFT — Sarah to write] The same traits that drive success — conscientiousness, high standards, commitment — make it harder to notice or admit when you are running on empty.

## What CBT tools work for burnout recovery

[DRAFT — Sarah to write] Boundary-setting, cognitive restructuring around perfectionism, and behaviour activation are three approaches that make a practical difference during recovery.
