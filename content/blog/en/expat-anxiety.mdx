[DRAFT — Sarah to write] Identity, isolation, language exhaustion, partner dynamics when one person is more settled than the other — expat stress has a particular texture that generic anxiety content rarely addresses.

## Why expat anxiety often goes unrecognised

[DRAFT — Sarah to write] From the outside, your life looks like an adventure. Naming the difficulty can feel ungrateful. That gap between appearance and inner reality is itself exhausting.

[DRAFT — Sarah to write] CBT is particularly useful for the thought patterns that expat life amplifies: catastrophising, perfectionism in a new language, and comparison with people who seem more adapted than you.
